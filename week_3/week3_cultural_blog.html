I remind myself a lot that the goal is growth not 'winning' at an assignment.

  • Kind feedback: Be nice. Regardless of what you have to say, you don't have to be mean about it.
  • Actionable: Make it something that can be implimented. It's very helpful to have concrete steps to take when trying to improve yourself. Actionable feedback should give you something that is clear what you can do to work on it.
  • Specific: Rather than giving a family of kind, actionable things as feedback. Focusing on one or a few things allows you to pick something that you can 'specifically' work on the next time. No need to fix the world right away.
